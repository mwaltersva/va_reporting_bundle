Students Served At Regional Centers

Counts students served at regional centers. Regional center division and school codes must be defined in the VRB config page.

  • October 1st - Fall SRC
  • December 1st - Dec 1 Special Ed child count
  • March 31st - Spring SRC
  • Last day of School - EOY SRC
