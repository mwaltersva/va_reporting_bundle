Students with Disabilities by Condition Type

Counts students with a primary disability who were active on the entered snapshot date. If no snapshot date is entered, the current date will be used. Usual snapshot dates include:

  • October 1st - Fall SRC
  • December 1st - Dec 1 Special Ed child count
  • March 31st - Spring SRC
  • Last day of School - EOY SRC
